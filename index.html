    About Me

    I am an aspiring UX designer with experience in designing new and redesigning old websites for the last six months in a bootcamp. I take pride in the work that I have done but I am always open to criticism to better myself and my work. I was originally studying to become a graphic designer but when I heard about UX design, I knew it would bring me more satisfaction and sense of accomplishment with every project that is worked on.

    My Roles

    Throughout past projects I have had many different roles. With my groups, we have always split or shared the roles of doing interviews, testing, cart sorting, making a user persona, sketches, wireframing, prototyping, testing more, iterating, and finishing a project. With each project my roles were different but no matter what, I found that the researching before building an app, website, or anything else is the most important.
