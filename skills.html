    User Research

    When conducting user research, I like to ask the least amount of questions but that have the most impact. Interviews can be more like conversations rather than robotic questions and answers.

    User Empathy

    I like to put myself in the shoes of the user persona, the people tested, and people in everyday life that could use the product. I want these people to feel connected to the product not only by what they see on the physical page but how they experience it.

    Photography

    I like to use imagery to portray a message. There are different feelings people can get just by a certain color or by an image that is placed so I want to make sure people get the “right” feeling that we are trying to portray.
